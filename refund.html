RETURNS AND REFUNDS POLICY

Last Updated: January 2025

OVERVIEW

At Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd., we understand that circumstances may arise where returns or refunds are necessary. This policy outlines the specific conditions under which we provide returns and refunds for our trading card products and break services.

Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase, as our refund policy is limited to specific circumstances outlined below.

CIRCUMSTANCES FOR RETURNS AND REFUNDS

Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd. will only provide returns and refunds under the following circumstances:

1. CANCELLED BREAKS OR GROUP BREAKS

Circumstance: A spot in a break or a group break is cancelled by Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd.

Our Response: Under this circumstance, the purchase will be credited to the customer or refunded to the original form of payment.

Details:

  • Full refund will be processed within 5-10 business days
  • Customer may choose between store credit or original payment method refund
  • Store credit does not expire and can be used for future purchases
  • Email notification will be sent confirming the cancellation and refund process

2. INCORRECTLY PROCESSED ORDERS

Circumstance: An order was incorrectly processed by Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd.

Our Response: Under this circumstance, Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd. will request the return of the incorrect package or items and cover the costs of shipping. Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd. will cover the cost of shipping the correct item.

Process:

  • Contact us immediately upon receiving an incorrect order
  • We will provide a prepaid return shipping label
  • Incorrect items must be returned in original condition and packaging
  • Correct items will be shipped at no additional cost to you
  • If correct items are unavailable, a full refund will be provided
  • Priority shipping will be used for replacement items when possible

3. CHANGE OF MIND/CHANGE OF TEAM

Circumstance: Change of mind/change of team by the customer prior to the break's occurrence*

Important Note: *Please Note: This circumstance is solely at the discretion of Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd. If this request was not responded and agreed by Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd. prior to the break's occurrence, a refund will not be provided.

Requirements:

  • Request must be made at least 24 hours before the scheduled break
  • Must receive written confirmation from Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd.
  • Limited to one change of mind per customer per month
  • May be subject to administrative fees depending on circumstances
  • Not available for limited edition or special promotional breaks

EXCLUSIONS - NO REFUNDS PROVIDED

Damaged or Lost Packages: Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd. will not provide refunds for any packages arriving to customers damaged or being lost in transit. Please refer to the SHIPPING POLICY.

Other Exclusions:

  • Break Results: No refunds based on the outcome or results of card breaks
  • Card Condition: No refunds for card condition issues that are considered normal for trading cards
  • Market Value: No refunds based on market value fluctuations of received cards
  • Personal Expectations: No refunds for breaks that don't meet personal expectations
  • Digital Products: No refunds for digital content once accessed or downloaded
  • Late Requests: No refunds for change of mind requests made after the break has occurred

REFUND PROCESSING TIMEFRAMES

Once a refund is approved, processing times vary depending on the payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days after approval
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days after approval
  • Bank Transfer: 5-7 business days after approval
  • Store Credit: Immediately available after approval

Please note that processing times may be longer during peak periods or holidays.

RETURN SHIPPING REQUIREMENTS

When returns are required, the following conditions apply:

  • Original Packaging: Items must be returned in original packaging when possible
  • Condition: Items must be in the same condition as received
  • Documentation: Include order number and return authorization number
  • Insurance: We recommend insuring valuable returns
  • Tracking: Always use trackable shipping methods for returns

PARTIAL REFUNDS

In certain circumstances, partial refunds may be provided:

  • When only part of an order is affected by our error
  • For items that cannot be returned due to condition
  • When shipping costs need to be deducted from refunds
  • For late cancellation requests (at our discretion)

DISPUTES AND CHARGEBACKS

Before initiating a chargeback or dispute with your payment provider:

  • Contact us first to attempt resolution
  • Allow sufficient time for our customer service team to respond
  • Provide all relevant documentation and details
  • Understand that chargebacks may result in account suspension

We are committed to resolving issues fairly and promptly when contacted directly.

AUSTRALIAN CONSUMER LAW

This policy operates in conjunction with Australian Consumer Law. You may have additional rights under Australian Consumer Law that cannot be excluded, including:

  • Right to repair, replace, or refund for major failures
  • Compensation for reasonably foreseeable loss or damage
  • Right to cancel a service that cannot be fixed

For more information about your consumer rights, visit the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission (ACCC) website.

POLICY UPDATES

This Returns and Refunds Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect:

  • Changes in business operations
  • Legal or regulatory requirements
  • Improvements to customer service
  • Feedback from customers

Material changes to this policy will be communicated via email to registered customers and prominently displayed on our website.

HOW TO REQUEST A RETURN OR REFUND

To request a return or refund in alignment with this policy, please contact us using one of the following methods:

Email: butlerbreaksboxes@gmail.com

Email: butlertradingcards@gmail.com

Contact Form: Use our website contact form

Response Time: We aim to respond within 24-48 hours

When contacting us, please include:

  • Your order number
  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Photos if applicable (damaged items, incorrect products)
  • Your preferred resolution (refund, exchange, store credit)
  • Contact information for follow-up

CUSTOMER SATISFACTION COMMITMENT

While our refund policy has specific limitations due to the nature of trading card breaks and collectibles, we are committed to:

  • Providing excellent customer service
  • Resolving issues fairly and promptly
  • Maintaining transparent communication
  • Continuously improving our products and services
  • Building long-term relationships with our customers

We appreciate your understanding of the unique nature of trading card breaks and collectibles, and we strive to provide the best possible experience for all our customers.
