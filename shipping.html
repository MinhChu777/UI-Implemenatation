SHIPPING POLICY

Last Updated: January 2025

INTRODUCTION

At Butler Trading Cards Pty Limited, we strive to provide prompt, consistent and reliable shipping services to our customers at a high quality. The Shipping Policy outlines how we handle shipping and delivery for customers.

We understand that receiving your trading cards promptly and in perfect condition is important to you. This policy details our shipping processes, costs, timeframes, and responsibilities to ensure you have clear expectations about your order delivery.

SHIPPING PROCESS AND CYCLE

The shipping process is performed entirely manual by the team at Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd. At the completion of each break, cards are sorted into teams, sleeved, and encased where required. These cards are arranged per customer in preparation for Shipping.

As such, electing for shipping will allow customers to have multiple breaks included in the same shipping cycle (within the fortnightly windows) with your cards.

Currently, the team at Butler Trading Cards Pty Ltd. complete shipping on a fortnightly cycle every second Friday.

Our Process:

  • Break Completion: Cards are sorted by team immediately after each break
  • Card Protection: All cards are professionally sleeved and encased where required
  • Customer Sorting: Cards are organized by customer for efficient packing
  • Quality Check: Each package is inspected before shipping
  • Batch Shipping: Orders are processed every second Friday
  • Consolidation Benefit: Multiple breaks can be combined in one shipment

SHIPPING SCHEDULE

Understanding our shipping schedule helps you plan when to expect your cards:

  • Shipping Days: Every second Friday
  • Processing Window: Up to 14 days from break completion
  • Consolidation Period: Multiple breaks completed within the same cycle will be shipped together
  • Holiday Adjustments: Schedule may be adjusted for public holidays
  • Notification: Customers will be notified of any schedule changes

SHIPPING METHODS AND PRICES

Shipping is not included in the cost of your purchase. Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd. offer various shipping methods to meet your needs through Australia Post Parcel Send. The available shipping options and estimated delivery times will be displayed at checkout. Delivery times are estimates and commence from the date of shipping, rather than the date of entry into a break.

Australia

  • Standard Shipping: $10.00 AUD*^. Estimated delivery within 3-7 business days for domestic orders.
  • Express Shipping: $13.50 AUD*^. Estimated delivery within 1-3 business days for domestic orders.

New Zealand

  • Standard Shipping: $18.00 AUD*^.
  • Express Shipping: $31.00 AUD*^.

For international orders, delivery times vary depending on the destination and shipping method selected.

Please note:

  • *Additional fees may be charged based on the weight and dimensions of the packages.
  • ^Insurance may be purchased at customer discretion and will be charged on-top of shipping costs (if applicable).

SHIPPING COST BREAKDOWN

Our shipping costs are designed to be transparent and competitive:

  • Base Shipping Rate: Covers standard packaging and postal service fees
  • Weight Surcharges: Applied for packages over standard weight limits
  • Dimension Surcharges: Applied for oversized packages
  • Insurance (Optional): Available for valuable shipments
  • Signature Required: Available for high-value orders

PACKAGING AND PROTECTION

We take great care in packaging your trading cards to ensure they arrive in perfect condition:

  • Individual Card Protection: Cards are sleeved in protective sleeves
  • Team Sets: Organized and secured together
  • Rigid Packaging: Bubble mailers or boxes for protection
  • Moisture Protection: Sealed packaging to prevent humidity damage
  • Fragile Handling: Clearly marked for postal service
  • Tracking Labels: Secure attachment for monitoring

ORDER TRACKING

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information. You can use this information to track the status of your shipment.

Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd. maintain a register of all tracking numbers for all customers for security purposes.

Tracking Information Includes:

  • Shipping confirmation email with tracking number
  • Direct link to Australia Post tracking
  • Estimated delivery date
  • Delivery status updates
  • Proof of delivery when available

DELIVERY TIMEFRAMES

Understanding delivery timeframes helps set proper expectations:

Domestic Australia

  • Metro Areas: 2-3 business days (Express), 3-5 business days (Standard)
  • Regional Areas: 3-5 business days (Express), 5-7 business days (Standard)
  • Remote Areas: 5-10 business days depending on location

International

  • New Zealand: 5-10 business days
  • Other Countries: 10-21 business days (varies by destination)

DELAYS AND ISSUES

In instances whereby Australia Post has been selected and once the postage has been sent, Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd. are not responsible in the event of packages arriving damaged or being lost in transit.

While we strive to ensure timely delivery of your packages, there may be delays due to factors beyond our control, such as postal service delays, customs processing, or natural disasters. Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd. are not responsible for delays caused by such external factors.

If your postage is delayed or you encounter any issues with the delivery, please CONTACT US, and we will do our best to assist you.

Common Delay Factors:

  • Australia Post service disruptions
  • Extreme weather conditions
  • Public holidays and peak periods
  • Customs processing for international orders
  • Incorrect or incomplete addresses
  • Remote delivery locations

LOST OR DAMAGED PACKAGES

While rare, packages may occasionally be lost or damaged during transit:

  • Reporting: Contact us immediately if you suspect loss or damage
  • Investigation: We will work with Australia Post to investigate
  • Insurance Claims: We assist with insurance claims where applicable
  • Documentation: Photos and detailed descriptions help with claims
  • Timeframes: Allow 10-14 business days for investigations

INCORRECT ADDRESSES

Please ensure that your shipping address is correct at the time of order placement. Butler Trading Cards Pty. Ltd. are not responsible for orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided by the customer. If an order is returned to us due to an incorrect address, we will contact you to arrange for re-shipment at your expense.

Address Verification Tips:

  • Double-check: Review address details before confirming order
  • Postal Codes: Ensure correct postcode for your area
  • Spelling: Check street names and suburb spelling
  • Unit Numbers: Include apartment/unit numbers where applicable
  • Contact Details: Provide current phone number for delivery

Re-shipment Process:

  • Returned packages will be held for 30 days
  • Customer will be contacted via email
  • New shipping costs apply for re-delivery
  • Address must be verified before re-shipment

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING

International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees that may apply to their shipment. These charges are not included in the purchase price or shipping costs and must be paid by the customer.

International Considerations:

  • Customs Duties: Vary by destination country and order value
  • Import Taxes: Applied by destination country customs
  • Brokerage Fees: May be charged by postal services
  • Delivery Delays: Customs processing can extend delivery times
  • Documentation: Accurate customs declarations provided
  • Prohibited Items: Some destinations may restrict trading cards

INSURANCE OPTIONS

Protect your valuable trading cards with optional shipping insurance:

  • Coverage Levels: Various insurance amounts available
  • Premium Calculation: Based on declared value of contents
  • Claims Process: We assist with insurance claims
  • Recommended For: High-value orders and rare cards
  • Peace of Mind: Protection against loss or damage

SPECIAL DELIVERY OPTIONS

Additional delivery options available for specific needs:

  • Signature Required: Ensures personal delivery
  • Authority to Leave: Allows delivery without signature
  • Safe Drop: Delivery to secure location
  • Parcel Locker: Delivery to Australia Post parcel lockers
  • Post Office Box: Delivery to nominated post office
