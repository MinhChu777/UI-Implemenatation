PRIVACY POLICY

Date Updated: 27/06/2024

OUR COMMITMENT TO YOUR PRIVACY

Butler Trading Cards Pty Limited ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Create an Account
  • Place an Order
  • Contact us via email or other communication methods

This information may include name, email address, postal address, contact information and payment information.

PERSONAL INFORMATION COLLECTION

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us through various interactions with our website and services. The types of personal information we may collect include:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, and password when you create an account
  • Order Information: Billing and shipping addresses, contact information, and payment details when you place an order
  • Communication Information: Any information you provide when contacting us via email, chat, or other communication methods
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, and other payment-related information necessary to process transactions

USE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

Butler Trading Cards Pty Limited may use the personal information collected for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing: To process and fulfill your orders, including to contact to confirm order status and shipment
  • Account Management: To manage your account and provide customer support
  • Communication: To communicate with you, including sending promotional materials and newsletters if you have opted-in
  • Service Improvement: To improve our website and services
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: To protect against and prevent fraud, unauthorised transactions, claims, and other liabilities

NON-PERSONAL INFORMATION

Butler Trading Cards Pty Ltd may use non-personal information to:

  • Website Enhancement: Improve our websites and service, including analysing trends to enhance user experience
  • Technical Support: Enhance our site and diagnose technical problems
  • Analytics: Understand how visitors use our website to improve functionality and user experience
  • Performance Monitoring: Monitor website performance and identify areas for improvement

Non-personal information includes data that cannot be used to identify you personally, such as browser type, device information, IP addresses (in aggregated form), and website usage statistics.

DATA SECURITY AND PROTECTION

We are implementing technical and security measures to protect the personal and non-personal information collected and processed. These measures include:

  • Encryption: We use industry-standard encryption protocols to protect sensitive data during transmission
  • Secure Storage: Personal information is stored on secure servers with appropriate access controls
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update our security measures to protect against new threats
  • Limited Access: Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Monitoring: We continuously monitor our systems for potential security vulnerabilities

While we implement robust security measures, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your information to the best of our ability.

ACCESS TO PERSONAL INFORMATION AND OPT-OUT REQUESTS

You have the right to access, correct or request to remove personal information gathered. Please update directly from your account details or contact us for assistance.

Additionally, upon request, you may opt-out of marketing communication from us through the 'unsubscribe' feature or by contacting us directly.

Your Rights Include:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and business requirements
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications at any time
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your personal information in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below.

COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These technologies help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements
  • Improve website functionality and performance

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We may use third-party services to help operate our website and business, including:

  • Payment Processors: To securely process payments and transactions
  • Shipping Partners: To fulfill and deliver orders
  • Analytics Services: To understand website usage and improve our services
  • Email Services: To send communications and newsletters

These third parties may have access to personal information necessary to perform their functions but are contractually obligated to maintain confidentiality and use the information only for specified purposes.

DATA RETENTION

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes for which it was collected
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Provide ongoing customer service

When personal information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it in accordance with our data retention policies.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

CHANGES TO THE PRIVACY POLICY

Please note that we may update the Butler Trading Cards Privacy Policy to reflect changes in operational, legal or regulatory requirements. When we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will:

  • Update the "Date Updated" at the top of this policy
  • Notify users via email or prominent website notice when appropriate
  • Provide a summary of significant changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information. Your continued use of our services after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
